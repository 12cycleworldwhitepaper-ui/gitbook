# Development Plan

12C's journey starts by turning ancient wisdom into on-chain data. It continues by allowing global users to turn their destinies into assets, eventually creating a grand cultural ecosystem through human connections and harmony.

## Phase 1: Genesis & Fair Launch (Q4 2025)

**Q4 2025 | Intellectual Property (IP) & Character Branding**

* **Fair Launch**: Official token issuance via 4Meme with transparent liquidity formation.
* **Genesis Community**: Launch of the global community and recruitment of Early Contributors.
* **Initial DEX Listing**: Following the completion of the 4Meme bonding curve, liquidity will be migrated and listed on major DEXs, such as PancakeSwap.

## Phase 2: Ecosystem Expansion & DApp Launch (2026)

**1. Web3 Destiny DApp ‘12C Compass’ Alpha**

* **DApp Launch**: Beta release of the 12C decentralized application. This marks the first step in assetizing destiny by allowing users to mint their Saju (Destiny) as a unique Soulbound Token (SBT) for on-chain identity verification.
* **Destiny Mapping**: Integration of ancient wisdom algorithms onto the blockchain to provide personalized daily insights and destiny-based asset management strategies.

**2. SocialFi Integration: "Matching & Compatibility"**

* **Compatibility Protocol**: Implementation of an on-chain compatibility (Gung-hap) system that analyzes users' data to recommend optimal partners for networking, collaboration, and social connections.
* **Connect-to-Earn**: Introduction of a SocialFi model that rewards users for engaging in meaningful community interactions and encounters based on their destiny data.

**3. Intellectual Property (IP) & Character Branding**

* **12 Zodiac Guardians**: Unveiling the "12 Zodiac Guardians" character IPs designed for a global audience. Each character features a unique lore synchronized with the user’s destiny data.
* **Character Licensing**: Expanding 12C IP into the mainstream market through partnerships with physical merchandise brands, digital fashion, and lifestyle labels.

**4. NFT Marketplace & Creator Economy**

* **Guardian NFTs**: Official minting of the 12 Zodiac Guardian NFTs, offering ecosystem utilities such as staking boosters and governance voting rights.
* **Creator Support Program**: Launch of a platform dedicated to supporting creators in producing secondary content, webtoons, and animations using 12C’s destiny-based IP.
